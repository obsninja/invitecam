Unlimited video quality

Due to the use of a hybrid peer-to-peer design, video call quality is not capped by the service. With a powerful computer and strong network connection, 8K video at over 60-mbps is achievable. A common use case is with ESports, where studios love the ability to pull smooth game feeds at 1080p60 from multiple professional gamers at a time with near zero-latency.

No downloads required

Performers only need a web-link to join a production; no downloads needed. Studios and producers have the option to leverage desktop applications or remain totally browser-based as well.

Trusted also by musicians around the world for its sound quality, latency, stereo support, and routing flexibility
